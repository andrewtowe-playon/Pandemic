OUTAGE SEASON

Cooperative  ·  2–4 Players

The Situation

It's game-day season and fires are breaking out across every domain — Platform, Payments, Security, and Support. Your team of specialists has to ship all four solutions before the company is overwhelmed. It's cooperative — you all win together, or you all lose together. Coordinate your roles and keep the escalations from spiraling out of control.

◆ How you win Ship solutions for all 4 domains (Platform, Payments, Security, Support).
◆ How you lose
  • The 8th escalation occurs.
  • You run out of capacity to respond.
  • The backlog runs out.
